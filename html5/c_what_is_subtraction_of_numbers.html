What is subtraction of numbers?

In this section, you will learn what subtraction is.

To perform the task of subtracting numbers, you need to know what subtraction is. "Subtraction" means taking something away from a group or number of things.

Subtraction represents a mathematical operation. It is a process that allows you to find the difference of two numbers.

You use the minus (-) button to express the subtraction operation in the app.
